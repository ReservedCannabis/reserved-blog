Storage & freshness

  • Use an air-tight container; avoid heat and direct light.
  • Humidity 58–62% RH for flower to preserve terpenes.
  • Label jars with date opened and target RH.

Beginner dosing

  • Keep a simple one-line journal: dose, time, effect.
  • Edibles: 1–2.5 mg THC; wait 2–4 hours before redosing.
  • Inhaled: 1–2 small puffs, wait 10–15 minutes, reassess.

How to choose

  • Match strength to goal. Start low, adjust weekly based on results.
  • Pick form factor for convenience: pre-roll, flower, vape, edible, oil.
  • Look at cannabinoids (THC vs CBD) and the top terpene listed.

Quick answers (FAQ)

How much should a beginner start with?
Start low and slow. For inhaled products, a couple of puffs; for edibles, 1–2.5 mg THC and wait at least 2 hours before taking more.

How do terpenes change effects?
Aromas like limonene (bright) or myrcene (relaxing) often shape the experience. Ask to smell jars if permitted.

What if I take too much?
Hydrate, rest, and give it time. CBD or black pepper aroma (beta-caryophyllene) can help some people feel more balanced.
