What is THC and CBD?

Before we delve into choosing the right THC vs CBD ratio, it's important to understand what these terms mean. Tetrahydrocannabinol (THC) and Cannabidiol (CBD) are the two primary active components of cannabis. THC is the component responsible for the psychedelic 'high' effect, while CBD has been found to counteract the effects of THC, offering a range of therapeutic benefits such as anti-inflammation and pain relief.


Understanding THC vs CBD ratios

THC vs CBD ratios refer to the amount of THC and CBD in a particular cannabis product. These ratios can be customized to harness the benefits of both components.


If you're dealing with a high level of discomfort or pain and your body needs a significant relaxation, a high THC vs CBD ratio of 20:1 or 10:1 might be right for you. These ratios provide the most potent psychoactive effects.


If you're after something a bit milder, ratios like 5:1, 2:1, or 1:1 might be the best option. They offer a balanced blend of both THC and CBD.


For those who prefer to enjoy the subtle benefits without the psychoactive effects, a high CBD to THC ratio, such as 20:1 or 30:1, will provide a calming, anxiety-relieving experience.
