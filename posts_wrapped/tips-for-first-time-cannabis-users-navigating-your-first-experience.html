Tips for First-Time Cannabis Users: Navigating Your First Experience

Embarking on your first cannabis experience can be exciting yet daunting; here are some tips to help you navigate it smoothly.

1. Start Low and Go Slow

When trying cannabis for the first time, it‚Äôs crucial to begin with a low dose, especially if you're using edibles or tinctures.
